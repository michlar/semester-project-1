EXHIBITIONS

Space Exploration

This exhibition on space exploration includes models of spacecrafts, simulations of space missions, and interactive displays on astronomy, planets, and the universe.

Human Body and Health

Explore human anatomy and physiology, with interactive displays on organs, the cardiovascular system, the brain, and the importance of leading a healthy lifestyle.

Technology and Innovation

Cutting-edge inventions and innovations. Includes displays on robotics, artificial intelligence, renewable energy, and the internet of things.

Biology and Biodiversity

Exhibition on biology and biodiversity to highlight the diversity of life on Earth. Includes displays on ecosystems, habitats, wildlife conservation, and the importance of biodiversity.

Dinosaur Discovery

Life-sized dinosaur skeletons, interactive displays on paleontology, and information about prehistoric ecosystems.
